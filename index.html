6) Common EV Leaks (Avoid)

  • Firing GD03-107 too early for low impact.
  • Trading tokens away without tempo/value gain.
  • Ignoring trash count setup for GD03-020 turns.
  • Forcing Breach before clearing blockers.
  • Overextending into one board reset.
